What Travelling to 30 Countries has Taught Me

Sara Moh Feb 10, 2023 3

Enjoying a peaceful morning in nature with a warm cup of coffee.

Travelling to different countries has been an incredible journey of self-discovery and personal growth. As an avid traveler who has explored 30 countries, I have been fortunate to experience diverse cultures, meet fascinating people, and witness breathtaking landscapes. Each trip has left an indelible mark on my soul, shaping my perspective and teaching me invaluable life lessons. In this article, I would like to share some of the profound insights I have gained through my adventures.


1. Embracing Diversity

One of the most important lessons I have learned is to embrace diversity. Every country I visited had its unique customs, traditions, and ways of life. By immersing myself in different cultures, I developed a deep appreciation for the beauty of diversity and learned to respect and celebrate our differences.


2. Overcoming Challenges

Traveling to unfamiliar places often presents unexpected challenges. From language barriers to navigating unfamiliar territories, I have faced numerous obstacles. However, these challenges have taught me resilience and adaptability.

I have learned to step out of my comfort zone, think on my feet, and find creative solutions to overcome hurdles.


3. Open-mindedness

Interacting with people from different cultures has broadened my perspective and challenged my preconceived notions. Through meaningful conversations and cultural exchanges, I have learned to approach life with an open mind and a willingness to learn from others. This open-mindedness has enriched my understanding of the world and made me more receptive to new ideas and experiences.


4. Gratitude

Traveling has instilled in me a deep sense of gratitude. Witnessing the hardships faced by people in various parts of the world has made me appreciate the privileges and opportunities I have been afforded. It has taught me to be grateful for the simple joys in life and to never take them for granted.


5. Living in the Present

Amidst the bustling streets of vibrant cities and the tranquility of natural wonders, I have learned the art of living in the present moment. Traveling has taught me to savor each experience, to be fully present, and to cherish the fleeting moments that make up our lives. It has reminded me to slow down, breathe, and appreciate the beauty that surrounds us.


Travelling to 30 countries has been an enriching and transformative journey for me. It has taught me invaluable life lessons, shaped my worldview, and opened my heart and mind to new possibilities. Through embracing diversity, overcoming challenges, cultivating open-mindedness, practicing gratitude, and living in the present, I have become a more compassionate, adaptable, and appreciative individual. My travels have not only provided unforgettable experiences but have also fostered personal growth and a deeper understanding of the world we inhabit.


"Travel makes one modest. You see what a tiny place you occupy in the world."

- Gustave Flaubert
