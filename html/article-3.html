How to Squeeze in More Reading Time While Traveling

Sara Moh Mar 28, 2023 3

A perfect holiday read by the beach. Relax, unwind, and get lost in the world of books.

As an avid traveler and book lover, I've discovered some effective strategies for maximizing my reading time during trips. In this article, I'll share my personal experiences and tips to help you make the most of your reading while on the go.


Traveling is a thrilling adventure that often leaves us with limited free time. However, with a few simple adjustments, you can find valuable moments to dive into the world of books. Here are some strategies I've found helpful:


1. Embrace Technology

Carry an e-reader or install a reading app on your smartphone or tablet. These portable devices allow you to carry a library of books in your pocket, ensuring you always have a captivating read at hand.


2. Utilize Downtime

Make use of the inevitable waiting times during your travels. Whether you're waiting at the airport, sitting on a train, or taking a break at a cafe, pull out your book and dive into a chapter or two. These stolen moments can quickly add up to significant reading progress.


3. Optimize Your Commute

If you're traveling by public transportation, make it a habit to read during your commute. Instead of scrolling through social media or playing games on your phone, use this time to immerse yourself in a captivating story or expand your knowledge through non-fiction.


4. Choose the Right Books

Select books that match the pace and mood of your travels. Engaging novels, short stories, or travel memoirs can transport you to different destinations and keep you engrossed in the reading experience. Consider picking books that align with the locations you'll be visiting, enhancing your overall travel experience.


5. Create Reading Rituals

Designate specific reading times during your trip. Whether it's in the morning with a cup of coffee or before bed to wind down, establishing a reading routine helps prioritize and dedicate time to this enjoyable activity.


Remember, the joy of reading is not limited to the destination but can be experienced anywhere.

By incorporating these strategies into your travel routine, you can maximize your reading time and create unforgettable memories through the pages of a book. So, pack your favorite novel, set off on your next adventure, and savor the moments of literary escape along the way!
